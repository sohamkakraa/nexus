# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Nexus a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for users and the community.
- Protect private data and report security concerns privately.

Harassment, sexualized behavior, insults, doxxing, threats, and publishing another person’s private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer through the contact options on [sohamkakra.com](https://sohamkakra.com). Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
